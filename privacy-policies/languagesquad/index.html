Privacy Policy for LanguageSquad

Last Updated: January 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to LanguageSquad. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We don't collect information

Permissions

  • Microphone: For audio-based language learning exercises
  • Apple Music: For playing educational audio content
  • Tracking: For personalized advertising (you can opt-out in iOS Settings)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our language learning services
  • Track your progress and maintain leaderboards
  • Display relevant ads in the free version (via Google AdMob)
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Respond to customer support inquiries

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use information about your device for personalized ads. Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy

Apple Services

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We receive purchase confirmations but not your payment information.

Data Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Account information
  • Game scores and progress
  • App preferences

Your Rights

  • Access: View your data within the App
  • Delete: Delete your account and data through App settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable ad tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Premium: Purchase "Remove Ads" to disable advertising

Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for users of all ages. We comply with COPPA and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information, except where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and providing in-app notification.
